Project Idea

Where can I watch that?

My project idea is for a website/app called “Where Can I Watch That?”. The purpose of this website/app is to enable users who travel frequently to still continue to watch a series or particular movie they may be following or watching at home while they are away.

The idea for my project comes from my own experience as I am a frequent traveller and often find myself in another country. I enjoy watching series on Netflix or other streaming platforms as a pastime and there have been more than a handful of times where I have been away and would like to continue watching a particular series I have been following, but when I go on Netflix or try to access my other streaming services in that region or country, they may not work there. I have also found that the bouquet offered on Netflix differs from country to country, meaning that even though a particular series is available when I’m home, the Netflix service in that country might carry a different selection of series and the one I am looking for is not on the list.

My project would thus be an app that allows you to search for a television series and/or movie while in a certain region which would show you what services are available to watch on in that particular country.

International travel has become much more common over the years both for leisure and business, and continues to grow. This can be seen by a study done by the World Travel Organisation, the research shows the increase in travel between 1950 and has predictions to 2030. It can be seen that in 1950 there was little or no regular travel but by 2030 international travel will increase to around 1.8 billion people travelling annually.

Thus there are millions of international travellers worldwide every day and they could potentially find themselves in a similar situation while they are abroad.

I have even run into the situation that, while I was away, I found and was enjoying a series, only to find that upon my return home and on the streaming platforms I subscribe to, the series is not available in their libraries. This situation can be very frustrating when looking for something you enjoy watching.

Another purpose that this app could be used for is to select the most suitable streaming service to subscribe to and which one fits your needs the best and offers what you are looking for. There are numerous different streaming services available in each country and the market is quite competitive and saturated and it could be confusing to select which one to choose with so many options available.
